### Realmz Classic Revival Project (Background Context)


The original Realmz had long since become abandonware, with no contact from creator Tim Phillips for years, and aside from a buggy 1999 Windows port and a now-obsolete Carbon-ization project from the 2010s, very little had been done to keep the game playable.
 
After research and outreach, the original creator Tim Phillips agreed to release the Realmz source code under a CC NonCommercial license, with a copy of the codebase obtained from the programmer who worked on the Carbon port.
 
The codebase was written in C under the c89 standard, with original Mac System 7 system calls needing to be stubbed and re-implemented.
 The beta release was announced in November 2025; this remains relevant as the most recent substantial classic Mac game source-code revival with ongoing community interest.

**Source:** [Realmz Classic Project](https://danapplegate.com/projects/2025/11/24/reviving-realmz.html)
